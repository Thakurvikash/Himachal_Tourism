About Langna

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Langna village is 013329. Langna village is located in Jogindarnagar Tehsil of Mandi district in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is situated 89km away from district headquarter Jogindarnagar. Jogindarnagar is the sub-district headquarter of Langna village. As per 2009 stats, Langna village is also a gram panchayat.

The total geographical area of village is 74.56 hectares. Langna has a total population of 525 peoples. There are about 117 houses in Langna village. Jogindarnagar is nearest town to Langna.
